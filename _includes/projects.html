VOXEL WAR

In this game you are Earth's defender who goes on a mission to save the planet from alien invaders! A first-person shooter based on a 1980 computer game called "Centipede". It's a fast-paced game where you have to focus on tactically shooting as many enemies as possible and as fast as possible, so they won't escape and cause mayhem.
